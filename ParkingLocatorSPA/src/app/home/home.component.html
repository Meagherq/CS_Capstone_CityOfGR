Let's Find You a Parking Spot! The Grand Rapids Parking Locator app helps you find available parking when traveling downtown. Locate spots by typing in the name or address of your desired location into the search bar. This app was made as a capstone project for Grand Valley's School of Computer Science together with the City of Grand Rapids.
